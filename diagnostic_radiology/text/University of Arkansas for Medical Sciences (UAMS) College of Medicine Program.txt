Additional Information Provided by Program
Our large volume of imaging studies, wide variety of pathology, well equipped training facilities and faculty committed to resident education make this training program an exceptional educational opportunity for residents. Our program is a 5-year categorical program that includes an integrated clinical year followed by 4 years of diagnostic radiology training. Diversity of workforce and emphasis on resident wellness are the other strengths of our program.
