Additional Information Provided by Program
We strive for both academic and professional excellence and are committed to a comprehensive education in all aspects of imaging-education through instruction and experience, using traditional workstation teaching and didactic and innovative teaching methods such as simulation and flipped classrooms. We perform more than 600,000 radiologic exams in total annually at our educational sites. All faculty are fellowship-trained with all subspecialties represented.
