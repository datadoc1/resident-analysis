Additional Information Provided by Program
Built-in TY year tailored for radiology for 6 candidates. Experienced faculty with impressive credentials to assist and mentor our residents during their training and to help expand their network when seeking post training employment. Faculty include many former PDs from other highly regarded institutions. The R4 year is almost entirely elective. Two residents each year may apply for ESIR. We also offer a nuclear medicine pathway. Mayo brand and world class faculty ensure resident success.
