Additional Information Provided by Program
The Diagnostic Radiology Residency Program at Baptist Hospital of Miami, will train the next generation of highly-talented radiologists who will serve the needs of patients within our diverse community and beyond while maintaining the highest level of excellence. Our smaller program will enable residents to work closely with sub-specialty radiologists in in-patient, outpatient and cancer institute settings.
