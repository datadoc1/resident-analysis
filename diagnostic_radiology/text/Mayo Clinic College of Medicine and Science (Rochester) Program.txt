Additional Information Provided by Program
The diagnostic radiology residency program at Mayo Clinic, Rochester, MN is an outstanding opportunity to work with 220 faculty interpreting over one million exams per year at a world renowned medical center. Program pays for one attendance meeting in addition to accepted presentations at scientific meetings. Residents have the option to rotate to the Phoenix Children's Hospital and to attend the AIRP with a travel stipend. Residents are given three paid fellowship interview days.
