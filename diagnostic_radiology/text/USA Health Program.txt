Additional Information Provided by Program
Our program provides a full spectrum of diagnostic imaging and interventional services. Being part of a Level 1 trauma center and major referral center in the Gulf Coast region for trauma, stroke, heart disease and other critical care conditions as well as having a Children's and Women's Hospital and the Mitchell Cancer Institute allows for advanced training in pediatric, women's and oncologic imaging.
