Additional Information Provided by Program
We offer a very strong training program in radiology which provides a supportive environment for residents. We also offer a guaranteed PGY1 preliminary position in our transitional year program to all matched applicants. All of our graduates go on to complete one or two fellowship programs in various subspecialties of radiology.
