Additional Information Provided by Program
Our program provides training at five different hospitals (Baylor St. Luke's Medical Center, Texas Children's Hospital, Ben Taub Hospital, the Michael E. DeBakey Veterans Affairs Medical Center, and the U.T. M.D Anderson Cancer Center located in the world's largest medical complex, the Texas Medical Center. Our resident are exposed to a vast variety of pathology and patient populations and are provided a great deal of autonomy in their training.
