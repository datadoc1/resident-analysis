Additional Information Provided by Program
UPMC provides a supportive and challenging curriculum that prepares you for a successful career in Radiology whether you choose a career in academics or private practice. You will get to see and do A LOT at UPMC. We have terrific fellowship placements and train some of the very best residents in the country. Residents receive 21 vacation days, 5 academic days, and awesome benefits including an employer paid healthcare plan and a very competitive salary.
