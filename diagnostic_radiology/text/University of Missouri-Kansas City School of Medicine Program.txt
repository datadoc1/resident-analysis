Additional Information Provided by Program
The University of Missouri-Kansas City radiology residency program has multiple training sites (including a busy tertiary care center, a dedicated children's hospital, a VA medical center, and a busy community hospital) that allow our residents to experience the entire breadth of radiology. We pride ourselves on providing training which allows our residents to obtain outstanding fellowships in their fields throughout the country. We also have an ESIR designation.
