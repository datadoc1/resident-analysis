Additional Information Provided by Program
We are a 4-year ACGME accredited program with an ESIR track. Residents gain competence, expertise, and become skilled, compassionate radiologists. Note, we: 1)Accept ERAS applications through 12/31 2)Require a preliminary clinical year in an ACGME accredited, AOA approved, RCPSC accredited or CFPC program, or ACGME-Int'l status program 3)Require minimum 230 USMLE Step I and II scores 4)Only sponsor J1 Visas 5) Interview Wednesdays, Oct 11 - Dec 27, 2023 www.downstate.edu/education-training
