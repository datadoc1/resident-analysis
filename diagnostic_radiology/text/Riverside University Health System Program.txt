Additional Information Provided by Program
RUHS Radiology is a recently formed clinical year integrated 5-year program with experienced faculty and ongoing accreditation. Our residents receive one on one teaching, faculty led didactic lectures and protected weekly Tuesday afternoon research time. Our level I trauma hospital serves a wide spectrum of patients with recently upgraded imaging equipment including PET/CT and MBI cameras.
