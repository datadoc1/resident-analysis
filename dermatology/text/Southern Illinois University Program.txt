Additional Information Provided by Program
We have weekly digital image conference, resident-led reading review and faculty-led lectures. We have a twice monthly journal club. We have monthly grand rounds sessions, basic science review, and melanoma multidisciplinary conference. We have a longitudinal derm surgery curriculum that starts PGY2 year. We have a 3 year rolling dermatoethics curriculum. And we have dedicated time for the pursuit of scholarly activity, as well as self-directed administration time for faculty and residents.
