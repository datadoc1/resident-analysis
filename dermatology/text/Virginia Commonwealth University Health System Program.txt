Additional Information Provided by Program
Our program excels at producing outstanding clinical dermatologists through exposure to a wide spectrum of diagnoses in a diverse patient population. VCU's experienced faculty hosts a robust orientation including a surgical skills seminar and dermatopathology boot camp, designed to advance your knowledge base and abilities. We pride ourselves on the quality support we provide; our entire department focuses on helping every resident meet milestone objectives and competencies.
