Additional Information Provided by Program
We offer a supportive teaching environment with diverse clinical settings servicing VA, private, community, and underserved patients. Our practice serves patients of all ages. We also provide Dermatology consultations for the University and VA hospital systems. We offer a very structured dedicated academic and dermatopathology curriculum. Though our focus is on general adult and pediatric medical and surgical/procedural training, we also have added laser training and regular cosmetic workshops.
