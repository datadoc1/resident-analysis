Additional Information Provided by Program
Silver Falls Dermatology is the first and only private practice in the Pacific Northwest to host a dermatology residency, one of the most prestigious and sought-after residencies in medicine. Our Dermatology Residency is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education (ACGME) and the American Osteopathic Association.
