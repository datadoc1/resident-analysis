Additional Information Provided by Program
Our program focuses on training residents in diagnosis and management of skin cancers. Residents enjoy hands on surgical training during residency. Faculty are engaged and 80% of conferences are faculty led. Residents and faculty enjoy an environment of mutual respect and supportive teaching. Generous stipends are provided for presentations at national meetings. Our board exam pass rate is 100%. Graduates pursue a mixture of private practice, academic dermatology and fellowship training.
