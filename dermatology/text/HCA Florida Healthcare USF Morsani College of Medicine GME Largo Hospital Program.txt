Additional Information Provided by Program
Our program offers unique surgical and cosmetic training with robust clinical and didactic content. Residents get elective rotation opportunities every year throughout training. We also have a terrific board preparation program that begins in the first weeks of residency. Our program offers linked Transitional Year/Dermatology positions: TY in 2023-2024 followed by Dermatology in the 2024-2025 academic year. NRMP codes for both are listed. Applications accepted on ERAS.
