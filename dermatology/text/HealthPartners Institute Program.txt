Additional Information Provided by Program
The HealthPartners Institute Dermatology Residency is an ACGME program located in the Twin Cities of Minnesota. We have more than 40 dermatologists as part of our department, many of whom have rich experience in teaching residents, including winning teaching awards. We value our belief that our resident physicians are our junior colleagues. We have a diverse patient population between our sites. The three most common languages spoken after English are Spanish, Somali and Hmong.
